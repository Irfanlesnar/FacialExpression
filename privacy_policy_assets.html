Privacy Policy for Spark AR Assets

At Spark AR Assets, we take the privacy of our users seriously. We understand that the information you provide to us is not that sensitive, but still, we are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.



Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us when you use our app, including your name, and Instagram profile. We also collect information about the files you upload through our App


How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide you with the best possible experience when using our app. We use your name to identify the creator of a file, and we use your Instagram profile to make people know about the creator. We may use your information to send you notifications about new features, and updates. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except as required by law.




How We Protect Your Information

We take several steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use secure servers and firewalls to protect your information, and we encrypt your information in transit. We also have strict access controls in place to ensure that only authorized personnel have access to your information.



Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We will not change this privacy policy. If we make any changes, we will notify you by posting the new policy on our app.
