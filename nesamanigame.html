Privacy Policy for Save Nesamani

At Save Nesamani, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game.



Information We Collect

Our game uses Google Sign-In through Firebase to authenticate users. We collect basic information from your Google account, such as your email address and display name. This information is used for leaderboard purposes and to personalize the game experience for you.


How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is primarily used to display your username on the leaderboard and save your progress within the game. We do not share or sell your data to any third parties.




Firebase and Google Sign-In

Save Nesamani integrates with Firebase services for user authentication and data storage. Google Sign-In is used to authenticate users and retrieve their profile information. Firebase securely stores your game progress and leaderboard rankings.




Advertising

Our game may display third-party advertisements. We do not share your personal data with advertisers. However, please note that advertisers may use cookies and other tracking technologies to show personalized ads.




How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure communication protocols and encryption where applicable.



Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We encourage users to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
